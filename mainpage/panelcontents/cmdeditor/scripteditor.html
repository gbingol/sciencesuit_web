Built-in Script Editor

Maybe you need more than few lines of commands to solve a problem. There is a script editor that is within the command editor to keep you focused on the task.

 

Say, you have started typing a command at the command prompt and realized that you would like to switch to the scripting mode. Just press Shift+Enter or Ctrl+Enter.

 

Needless to say but you can browse the history of the scripts and save all your scripts as well.
